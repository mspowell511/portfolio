Who am I?

As a designer, and artist, one is often shocked to learn that I am colorblind. However, this condition that I have is what sets me apart from others, and is often what leads me to creating things the way I do. When attempting to create something in real life, it can be difficut for me to show the real colorization. Due to this, I attempt to utilize alternative color choices, or colors that create a mood. If not focusing on color, I attmept to use line work to create a clean look, and occassionally portray something in a way no one has envisioned before. My portfolio consists of a variety of mediums, all influenced by how I see things in the word. By literally, and figurativley, seeing the world different from the rest I hope to make people reconsider how they see things. I want to influence others to look at an ordinary object and envision it in an extrordinary way. I am currently enrolled at Carnegie Mellon University's School of Architecture as a First Year student. I am interested in a Minor in Photography as well. I look forward to continually meeting new people who can help further enhance my technical skills, as well as myself as a person.
